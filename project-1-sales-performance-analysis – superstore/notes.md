## Dataset Overview
- Number of rows : 9994
- Number of columns : 20
- Time period : 2014 - 2017
- Key columns :
    - Sales
    - Profit
    - Category/Sub-Category
    - Region/State
    - Discount

## Business Questions
- How has sales performance changed over time?
- Are there any product categories with high sales but low profitability?
- Which states demonstrate the strongest sales performance?
- Which sub-categories contribute the most to total sales (Top 10)?
- What is the relationship between discount levels and sales performance?
- How do sales and profit compare across different sub-categories?
