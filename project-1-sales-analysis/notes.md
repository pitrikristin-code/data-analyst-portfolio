## Dataset Overview
- Number of rows: 9994
- Number of columns: 21
- Time period: 2/1/2014 - 9/30/2017
- Key columns:
    - Date
    - Sales
    - Profit
    - Region
    - Category
    - Subcategory

## Initial Observations
- The dataset spans multiple years with transactions recorded at a daily level.
- Furniture and Office Supplies appear frequently across the dataset.
- Some orders show negative profit values.
- Discounts vary across products and orders.
- Sales values range widely, indicating both small and large transactions.
- The dataset includes multiple customer segments and regions.
